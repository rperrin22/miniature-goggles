About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I'm Rob, and this seems fun. I don't really have much to teach that you couldn't learn from others. Everywhere I look, I see experts in every topic imaginable. I'm not one of them. I'm just bumbling my way through life, trying to make it work, without taking it too seriously.

I'm a 20 year old living in a 40 year old body who, for some reason, decided that it would be fun to go back to grad school. Lucky for me, I have a wonderfully supportive family who are willing to put up with me messing around a bit to try to figure out what I'm doing with my life when it seems like everyone else is out there conquering the world.

These are some of my stories. Don't expect words of wisdom from someone who's got it all figured out. Instead, you'll get the view of a pleeb who's poking his nose where it might not belong.
